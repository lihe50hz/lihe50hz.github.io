Personal Suggestions

  • If you want to learn some math courses outside of your major, sit in on the class rather than select it in the course registration system. Ask TAs for the homework and do it on your leisure.
  • Start sunshine running as early as possible.
  • Finish the homework at least two hours ahead of the deadline.
  • Don't be shy. Social is as important as studying.
